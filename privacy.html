Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Indigo Apps Co (“we”, “our”, “us”) handles information when you use our mobile apps, including GPS Camera and Zoomify.

What we collect

  • Device permissions you choose to grant (for example: camera, storage, location, microphone). Permissions are requested by the app only when needed for features.
  • Basic app diagnostics (such as crash information and performance signals) to improve stability and fix bugs.
  • Support messages if you email us — we receive the content you send and your email address so we can reply.

How we use information

  • Provide app features you request (e.g., stamping photo metadata, editing/enhancing photos).
  • Maintain reliability, troubleshoot issues, and improve performance.
  • Respond to support requests and user feedback.

Photos and content

  • Your photos are processed on your device unless a specific feature clearly states otherwise.
  • We do not sell your personal data.

Third-party services

  • Our apps may use trusted third-party services (for example, Google Play services, analytics, or advertising) that may collect data under their own privacy policies.
  • Where required, we rely on platform consent tools and app permission prompts.

Your choices

  • You can control permissions anytime in your device settings (deny/allow camera, location, microphone, etc.).
  • Some features may not work without certain permissions.

Data retention

  • We keep support emails only as long as needed to resolve your request.
  • Diagnostic data is retained for a limited period to improve stability and prevent repeated crashes.

Children

  • Our apps are not directed to children under 13 (or the age of digital consent in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
